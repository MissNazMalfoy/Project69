Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

St. Xavier's High School, Fort, is a private Catholic primary and secondary school for boys located in Fort, Mumbai, India. The English medium school was founded in 1869 and is run by the Society of Jesus.



School Library

Was built in 1980

The college facilitates a well-stocked library with collection of more than eighteen thousand books on different subjects. International and National Journals and Magazines from various disciplines are also available.



School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

Extensively used by the students for extra-curricular activities and for hosting functions of the College, SRCC auditorium has a seating capacity of about 750 people. It has an advanced light and sound system that is managed by specially designated support staff along with the students. It is equipped with a large stage, one green rooms, wall paneling for acoustic effects, air conditioning and podium.
